Meet Our Culinary Experts

The masters behind the magic of Annapurna.

Chef Rajesh Kumar

Executive Head Chef

With over 20 years of experience across luxury hotels in India, Chef Rajesh is the mastermind behind our signature North Indian curries. His specialty is the legendary Dal Makhani, slow-cooked overnight to perfection.

Chef Ananya Singh

Tandoor Specialist

Chef Ananya trained in the heart of Punjab. She commands the clay ovens at Annapurna, ensuring every Naan is perfectly crisp on the outside and soft on the inside, and every Tikka is smoky and tender.

Chef Vikram Das

Biryani Master

Hailing from Hyderabad, Chef Vikram treats Biryani not just as food, but as art. With 15 years of dedication to the 'Dum' cooking method, he carefully balances saffron, spices, and premium basmati rice.

Chef Meera Patel

Street Food & Chaat

Bringing the vibrant flavors of Mumbai street food to a fine-dining setting. Chef Meera spent a decade perfecting the exact tangy, spicy, and sweet balance required for our famous Samosas and Chaats.

Chef Rohan Iyer

Pastry & Dessert Chef

No meal is complete without dessert. Chef Rohan blends traditional Indian sweet-making techniques with modern presentation, crafting the softest Gulab Jamuns and the creamiest Rasmalai you will ever taste.
